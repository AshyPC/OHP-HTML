Rej 243: Lord Enthroned in Heavenly Splendour

1. Lord, enthroned in heavenly splendour, glorious first-born from the dead, You alone our strong defender lifting up Your people's head: Hallelujah! Hallelujah! Jesus, true and living bread!
2. Prince of life, for us now living, by Your body souls are healed; Prince of peace, Your pardon giving, by Your blood our peace is sealed: Hallelujah! Hallelujah! Word of God in flesh revealed.
3. Paschal Lamb! Your offering finished, once for all, when You were slain; in its fullness undiminished shall forevermore remain: Hallelujah! Hallelujah! Cleansing souls from every stain.
4. Great High Priest of our profession, through the veil You entered in, by Your mighty intercession grace and mercy there to win: Hallelujah! Hallelujah! Only sacrifice for sin.
5. Life-imparting heavenly manna, stricken rock, with streaming side; heaven and earth, with loud hosanna, worship You, the Lamb who died: Hallelujah! Hallelujah! Risen, ascended, glorified! G Bourne 1840-1876, Rejoice! © Presbyterian Church Australia
