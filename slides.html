Rej 150: We Greet You, Sure Redeemer

1. We greet You, sure Redeemer from all strife, our only Trust and Saviour of our life, who pain did undergo for our poor sake; we pray You from our hearts all cares to take.
2. You are the King of mercy and of grace, reigning omnipotent in every place: so come, O King, and our whole being sway; shine on us with the light of Your pure day.
3. You are the life, in which we do believe, from You all substance and all strength receive; sustain us by Your faith and by Your power, and give us strength in every trying hour.
4. You have the true and perfect gentleness; You have no harshness and no bitterness: O grant to us the grace in You we see that we may dwell in perfect unity.
5. Our hope is in no other save in You; our faith is built upon Your promise true; Lord give us peace and make us calm and sure, that in Your strength we evermore endure. John Calvin 1509-1564, Rejoice! © Presbyterian Church Australia
